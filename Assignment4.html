Fees and payments

Stuartholme has an all-inclusive fee policy.
The fee covers the cost of tuition, textbooks
, technology licenses and software, year level camps and retreats, compulsory in-class
excursions, alumnae membership, P&F levy,
and a number of miscellaneous expenses such as class photo packs.


The tuition fee does not include stationery
laptop, specialised equipment/materials,
optional co-curricular sports and activities,
co-curricular camps and tours, international
and domestic exchange programs or overseas
tours, all of which are parent’s responsibility
and therefore paid for separately.

A renowned boarding school
with moral and academic excellence


Outreach is a renowned boarding school in the heart of ikeja, Lagos. We pride ourselves on providing a high school education, fostering creativity, identity and confidence. We strive to ensure a nurturing environment for all students across all year levels, to equip them with all the knowledge to pursue their aspirations in the present and later in life.


As one of Lagos’s finest day and boarding school, we instil in our students the values of respect, integrity and honesty. We inspire the girls of OutReach to become confident high-level thinkers and aspiring global citizens beyond the gates of our private campus.


Our boarding program fosters a sense of family, belonging and inclusiveness. Students can board with us all year around, or on a term by term basis. The boarding facilities are located on our private school grounds next to the pristine natural parkland.
